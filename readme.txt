Purpose
=======

This is a very simple extension that adds a Facebook Like Box as a module to your Opencart installation. 

There are not a terrible lot of options, but on the other hand, it's terribly easy to use. Just provide your Facebook page URL, provide dimensions of the box and add the module to layout(s) and you are done.
